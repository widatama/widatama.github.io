Hipster Penguin

Hipster Penguin is the administrator part for HijUp. It was created as a platform so that, in the future, any brands that is currently hosted in HijUp could also manage their own online store.

Hipster Penguin was launched in 2013. I was in charge of front-end development, visual design, information architecture and user experience.

Running the daily activities for an online store like HijUp could be overwhelming. Add to that the fact that our team is really small. For this reason we strive for simplicity, consistency and clarity in Hipster Penguin to achieve maximum efficiency.
