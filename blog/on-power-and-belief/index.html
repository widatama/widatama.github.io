…for what is it that directs the minds of the powerful? The anwer is “Belief”. Beliefs that are ignoble or idealistic; democratic or Confucian; Occidental or Oriental; timid or bold; clear-sighted or delusional. Power is informed by Belief that this path, and not another, must be followed.

The Thousand Autumns of Jacob de Zoet by David Mitchell
