Peppertones

31 December 2012 • in Music 

Peppertones (Hangul: 페퍼톤스), is a Korean electronic/house duo. I don’t usually listen to Korean music but this one is hard to resist. At first I only listen to their English songs because I do not understand Korean, but then I tried listening to some of the Korean songs and I am hooked. I can listen to their fun and catchy tunes all day long at work, err while working that is.
