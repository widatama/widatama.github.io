Minimalism

3 March 2013 • in Writing 

I have been frequenting this subreddit lately and I found it really fascinating. I did not know why but this idea of minimalism just felt profound. There was a certain sense of tranquility that I get by seeing all those posts (yes, those pictures of clean desks included).

What I am really interested though is applying minimalism to our life. The idea is simply brilliant. Reduce everything to only what is necessary. This way, we can focus on the most important things and hence get the most out of our life.
